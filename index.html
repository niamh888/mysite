Welcome to My Research Site

This is an introduction to my research and standards' work performed as part of DkIT's Regulatory Software Research Centre

My PhD research focuses on the development of regulatory requirements for Artificial Intelligence-enabled Medical Devices (AIeMD). In particular, Change Management of AIeMD

As part of my research, I am developing a framework for the management of change in AIeMD, which includes a set of standards and guidelines for the development and maintenance of AIeMD. I am also developing a set of tools and techniques for the management of change in AIeMD, which includes a set of software tools and techniques for the management of change in AIeMD.

Research Interests

My research interests include the development of regulatory requirements for AIeMD, the management of change in AIeMD, and the development of standards and guidelines for the development and maintenance of AIeMD.
